About Us

Know us a little bit more.

Company Profile

Smartcomp Solutions, Inc. has established as one of the leading players in the market for IT-based solutions geared towards the branch operations of banks , retails and PC Industry. It Started as Sycom Systems and Services, Inc. in March 1,2001 with SM Dept. Store Branches as first client (Resident Engineers for Point of Sales – POS for all SM Branches ).

Vision

To achieve total customer satisfaction through innovative solutions and high quality services.

Attributes

Innovation leadership, responsibility and passion.

Mission

Our Customers are the highest priority. Our people are the greatest asset of the company.
